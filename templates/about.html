About PWD Assistant

Fostering digital inclusion through accessible technology.

Our Mission

The PWD Assistant project addresses the critical challenge of scattered and inaccessible information about facilities and services available to Persons with Disabilities (PWD).

Our goal is to be a centralized, multilingual platform where PWD candidates can easily access reliable information across key domains like education, healthcare, and government schemes, powered by an intelligent AI assistant.

Key Features

Centralized Information: All government schemes, benefits, and support services in one place.

AI-Powered Chatbot: Instant, accurate responses to queries about disabilities and available support.

Accessible Design: Full support for voice input, text-to-speech, and screen readers.

Community Forum: Connect with others, share experiences, and exchange resources.
